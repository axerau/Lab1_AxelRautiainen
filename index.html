It's so nice to see you here, visiting my website.

My name is Axel Rautiainen, and I am a 23 year old guy from Norrköping who currently studies at Luleå University of Technology. More specifically, I am in my final year of studying for a Master's degree in Space and Atmospheric Science and Engineering at the Kiruna Space Campus. Besides space, I like playing board games, video games, and hanging out with friends. I also love being out in nature, and since moving to Kiruna I have gone on beautiful hikes in Abisko with my friends on multiple occasions.
